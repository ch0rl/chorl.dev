Email Disclaimer

Content

Content of any communications are for the recipients only and may be privileged/confidential, and should be treated as such. If you are not the intended recipient, please let me know immediately and delete the message.

Attachments & Links

Any attached files are not guaranteed to be free of malware, viruses, etc., and are downloaded and opened at your own risk. Similarly, I cannot take responsibility for the content of any sites linked to. Of course, I would not intentionally send malware to anyone, or link to sites that I had not vetted, but this disclaimer still stands.

Tone

As I am autistic, I struggle to convey tone properly; I, therefore, ask that you assume the best and read any message from me as being light-hearted. I rarely, if ever, message anyone with accusatory/negative/harsh intent, so it is safe to assume I do not mean to come across that way. Where possible (i.e., not in formal communications), I will endeavour to use tone tags/indicators to clarify tone.

Timings

I tend to work at varying times throughout the day — sometimes 1200, sometimes 0300 — so, if I do respond at some random time, please do not think I am expecting a similar response. If you want to reply at some random time as well, be my guest, but I'm certainly not expecting it.
