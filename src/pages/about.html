Maths

I tend to enjoy maths problems that are “over the top” and tend to stem from “what if...” type conversations. For example:

  • If two masses are placed in a vacuum, how long would it take – due to their gravitation alone – for them to collide? (As a function of their masses and initial positions.)1
  • If a beam of light is “shot” along a plane, with constant gravity, will it ever hit the plane? (If so, when?)
  • How far away is a storm, given the time difference between lightning and thunder being observed?
